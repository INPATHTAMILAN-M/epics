Guidelines for EPICS 2025

  • EPICS 25 is exclusively open to first-year and second-year students.
  • Each team must consist of 3 to 4 members.
  • At least one female student must be included in each team.
  • Teams may be formed across different departments.
  • A student can be a member of only one team.
  • All problem submissions must be made using the official college email ID.
  • Plagiarism, including copying ideas or content from the internet or AI tools such as GPT, is strictly prohibited.
